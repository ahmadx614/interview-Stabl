Stabl - Voices from the Gym

Hear what fitness enthusiasts say about the need for a Made in India gym shoe brand

Rishabh, 24 – Delhi, Fitness Coach

"Honestly, like most people I train just wear running shoes or basic sneakers at the gym. There’s no popular gym-specific shoe brand that comes to mind. If there was a legit Made in India brand engineered just for gym performance, I’d definitely try it out—it’s long overdue."

Ayesha, 22 – Pune, College Student

"I usually wear Vans to the gym—they look good but I don’t think they’re meant for lifting since they’re worn out already. I don’t think I’ve seen any Indian brand focused only on gym shoes. If one existed and had a cool design with proper support, I’d switch instantly."

Omkar, 27 – Mumbai, Corporate Professional

"I have been training for around 10 years. It’s always Nike Romaleos or Reebok Nanos, but they’re extremely expensive and hard to find here. An Indian brand making proper gym shoes would be awesome, especially if they understood what lifters and cross-trainers actually need with the right budget."

Sakshi, 30 – Delhi, Pilates Instructor

"Most of the shoes people wear aren’t stable at all—no heel support, no grip. If someone made a shoe in India that’s functional and stylish, I’d recommend it to all my clients. It’s a huge gap in the market."

Harsh, 18 – Pune, Beginner Lifter

"I just wear my running shoes to the gym... didn’t know there’s a difference. But yeah, if a brand specifically focused on gym shoes and explained why they’re better for lifting, I’d check it out. Especially if it’s Made in India."

Devika, 21 – Mumbai, Fitness Content Creator

"There’s literally no Indian brand I can tag or promote for proper gym shoes—everything’s either foreign or lifestyle wear. If Stabl nails performance and looks clean on camera, trust me, there’s a whole market ready for it, also please make the box 'Instagrammable'."

Aman, 33 – Delhi, National Weightlifting Athlete

"Right now I import my lifting shoes—they’re way overpriced here. We need something local that gets the technical side right: ankle support, sole grip, heel height. If STABL does that, I’d be your first customer."
